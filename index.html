This experience laid a solid foundation in programming and computational thinking, while also broadening my perspective on the potential of technology to make meaningful contributions to healthcare and beyond.

Transitioning from the nuanced realm of molecular bionics to the dynamic world of iOS application development, and now to data science, my career trajectory might seem unconventional.
